About HQS Logistics

A trusted Amazon DSP, built in Jeddah

Our goal is simple: deliver packages safely and on time to families and communities across Jeddah as a trusted Amazon Delivery Service Partner. A great delivery starts with our drivers — so we support our team on the road, treat them with respect, and build reliable jobs they can count on.

We're here to make everyday life easier for our customers, one delivery at a time — and to grow steadily alongside Amazon as e-commerce expands across the kingdom.

Sarah AlHaqas — Owner & Managing Director
